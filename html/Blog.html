Interviews with Sustainable Brands & Experts

Interviews with sustainable brands and experts highlight themes like transparency, circularity, and fighting greenwashing, with experts stressing honest communication, showing improvement areas, and innovative materials (Tencel, recycled textiles) over perfection. Brands and hotel groups focus on lifecycle analysis, reducing impact (water, waste), and embedding sustainability into core business,while experts advise on focusing efforts, collaborating, and using certifications for credibility.
